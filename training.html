Youth Protection Training

Required for all new registered adults and must be retaken every two years to maintain registration. Can be taken at MyScouting.

Troop Committee Challenge

Required for all committee members. Can be taken at MyScouting.

Scoutmaster Position Specific Training

Required for the Scoutmaster and all Assistant Scoutmasters. An instructor led course run by Monmouth Council. Check for the next scheduled course at: Monmouth Council - Trainings.

Introduction to Outdoor Leader Skills (IOLS)

Required for the Scoutmaster and all Assistant Scoutmasters. An instructor led course run by Monmouth Council. Check for the next scheduled course at: Monmouth Council - Training.

Supplemental Training

Additional trainings are available both online at MyScouting as well as instructor led courses run by Monmouth council. See the list here: Monmouth Council - Training.

Den Chief Training

All scouts wishing to serve as Den Chiefs should complete online training immediately at BSA Training - Youth and the instructor led course at the next offering. See the list here: Monmouth Council - Training.

Upon Completion of Courses

Printed copies of certificates for all required training must accompany adult applications. Electronic copies of all training should be sent to (training@bsatroop86.org).
